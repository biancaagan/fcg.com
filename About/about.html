About



Frequency Coordination Group, LLC


The Frequency Coordination Group provides onsite technical support & frequency coordination of wireless audio system for broadcast, theater, concerts, festivals, & House of Worship.

The FCG team has over 25 years of expertise in the live audio and broadcast industry - from Super Bowls & Lollapalooza to corporate meetings & Broadway shows.

No matter the size or application, Frequency Coordination Group has the team to provide an interference-free, rock solid performance during your event.
